What are editing notes? Read till the end...

An Editor's Note is common feature of a press release. It is placed at the bottom of the press release and contains background information about the person who wrote it. It is an excellent way to provide a reporter with background information about the author, as well as the company that the person is reporting on.

Can notes be permanently deleted? Read till the end...

When you delete a note, it's moved to the Recently Deleted folder on iCloud.com and on your devices that are signed in with the same Apple ID. You can view and recover notes in the Recently Deleted folder for up to 30 days before they're permanently removed from all your devices.
