Bootstrap Deleting the notes

Why notes are deleted in notes app?

Using a series of notes in notes app, you can delete the notes whenever you longer need that saved notes.

Where are deleted notes moved?

Notes which are deleted permanently whenever user clicks on delete button.

Do Notes delete permanently?

Android. To permanently delete individual notes, open the note in the Trash, tap the options button (three dots), then tap Delete note forever. To empty the entire trash, open the Trash, tap the More actions button (three dots), then tap Empty trash.
